Our Services

At Helix Craftworks, precision is in our DNA. We take pride in offering high-quality remodeling and handyman services that combine craftsmanship and innovation. With over 20 years of experience, we deliver tailored solutions to transform your space, whether it’s a small upgrade or a complete renovation.

Interior Remodeling

  • Kitchen Remodeling: Transform your cooking space with custom cabinets, modern countertops, and smart layouts.
  • Bathroom Renovations: Upgrade your bathroom with new fixtures, tile work, and functional design.
  • Custom Built-Ins: Maximize space and add character with handcrafted built-in shelves, entertainment centers, and storage solutions.
  • Hidden Doors and Secret Spaces: Add a touch of mystery and style with concealed doors that blend seamlessly with your interior.

Electrical Services

  • Interior Wiring: Update or install wiring to meet modern standards while enhancing safety and reliability.
  • Lighting Installation: From recessed lighting to decorative fixtures, brighten up your space with expertly installed lighting.
  • Smart Home Integration: Seamlessly incorporate smart technology into your home for added convenience and energy efficiency.
  • Service Panel Upgrades: Improve electrical capacity to accommodate new appliances and modern devices.

Trim and Finish Carpentry

  • Crown Molding and Baseboards: Elevate the look of any room with precisely cut and installed trim.
  • Window and Door Casings: Add a polished finish to your space with expertly crafted casings.
  • Custom Woodwork: Unique, handcrafted details that set your space apart.

Exterior Carpentry and Wiring

  • Deck and Patio Enhancements: Enhance outdoor living with sturdy, stylish decking and efficient lighting.
  • Exterior Lighting: Increase safety and ambiance with professionally installed outdoor fixtures.
  • Garage and Workshop Wiring: Ensure your workspace is well-lit and powered for any project.

Your satisfaction is our priority. Whether it’s a minor repair or a major renovation, Helix Craftworks delivers exceptional quality and attention to detail. Contact us today for a consultation and let’s bring your vision to life!
