Projects That Whisper, Wow, and Occasionally Hide a Secret Door

Every Helix Craftworks™ project is a little bit mythical and a lot bit meticulous. From custom kitchen revivals to full-blown bookshelf bunkers with hidden passageways, we don’t just remodel spaces—we reimagine how they *feel* to live in.

Below is a sampling of the kinds of spaces we've transformed, restored, and secretly rigged with magnets. Want to see yours here someday? Get in touch.

The Hidden Library
A full wall-to-wall bookshelf with a secret: a disguised entry into a private study. Crafted with seamless trim, precision mechanics, and just a pinch of literary mischief.
Specialty: Hidden door carpentry, grain alignment, bespoke trimwork.
Modern Rustic Kitchen Revival
Goodbye drywall cave. Hello open kitchen with handcrafted walnut range hood, new electrical, and open shelving with character.
Specialty: Custom cabinetry, live-edge integration, fine electrical.
The Wall that Wasn't
Appears solid—until it swings open to reveal a hidden wardrobe. This flush-mount bookcase wall fakes you out in the best way possible.
Specialty: Magnetic latches, precision reveal work, secret closet vibes.
Colonial Staircase Restoration
Old staircase, new swagger. Solid oak treads, custom rails, and historical color matching to keep the soul of the home intact.
Specialty: Custom stair fabrication, historical detailing, structural revival.
The Basement Speakeasy
Behind the utility closet: a hidden lounge and wet bar with live-edge maple, ambient lighting, and no password required.
Specialty: Electrical concealment, reclaimed woodwork, bar craftsmanship.
